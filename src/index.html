Search Syntax

More words better results sort.
Examples : 
physical characteristics humanoid
Egyptian pyramids power

You can use operators OR AND NOT (capital letters) to improve your search.
Examples : 
alien AND vessel
krulian OR pleiadian
alien AND NOT pleiadian

You can use double quotes to a exactly match.
Example, to search just questions with night and walkers together with a space in the middle : 
"night walkers"

You can use * symbol to partial search.
Example : 
pleiadi*

You can use parenteces to a complex search.
Example: 
(chithok AND pleiades)
OR (pleiades AND krulians)
